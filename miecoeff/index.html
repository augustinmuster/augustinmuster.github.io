Mie Coefficients

MieCoeff is a small module for computing Mie Coefficients. It also contains some useful function to compute cross sections from the Mie theory.

Expressions are taken in Craig F. Bohren, Donald R. Huffman, Absorption and Scattering of Light by Small Particles (1998).

Functions Documentation

Main.CoupledElectricMagneticDipoles.MieCoeff.mie_an — Method
mie_an(ka, eps, eps_h; mu=1, mu_h=1, n=1)

Computes the n-th mie coefficient $a_n$ of a sphere with dimensionless radius ka, and of dielectric permittivity and magnetic permeability eps and mu, in a host medium with dielectric permittivity and magnetic permeability eps_h and mu_h. Returns a complex scalar.

Main.CoupledElectricMagneticDipoles.MieCoeff.mie_extinction — Method
mie_extinction(ka,eps,eps_h;cutoff=20)

Computes the extinction efficiency $Q_{ext}$ of a sphere with dimensionless radius ka, and of dielectric permittivity and magnetic permeability eps and mu, in a host medium with dielectric permittivity and magnetic permeability eps_h and mu_h. For this, we use:

\[Q_{ext} =\frac{2}{ka^2}\sum^{\infty}_{n=1}\left(2n+1\right)Re\left(a_n+b_n\right)\]

The infinite sum is computed only for terms under the cutoff variable (by default set to 20).

Returns a float.
